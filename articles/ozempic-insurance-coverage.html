Step 3: Get Prior Authorization

Most insurers require prior authorization for Ozempic. Here's how to get approved:

  1. Have your doctor submit documentation showing medical necessity
  2. Include lab results: A1C levels, blood glucose readings
  3. Document failed treatments: Show that diet, exercise, or other medications didn't work

Denials aren't final. You have the right to appeal, and many appeals succeed:

  2. Have your doctor write a letter of medical necessity
  3. Gather supporting documentation: Lab results, medical history, previous treatment failures
  4. Submit within the deadline (usually 30-60 days)
