Ozempic Cost Without Insurance

The manufacturer's list price for Ozempic (semaglutide) is $997.58 per month in the United States. At retail pharmacies, you'll typically pay between $935 and $1,200 depending on the pharmacy and your location.

Ozempic Cost With Insurance

If your insurance covers Ozempic, the cost drops dramatically:

  • With manufacturer savings card: As low as $25/month
  • Typical copay range: $25 - $100/month
  • High-deductible plans: Full cost until deductible is met

Why Is Ozempic So Expensive?

Several factors contribute to Ozempic's high price:

  • No generic available: Novo Nordisk holds the patent until 2031
  • High demand: Celebrity endorsements drove massive demand
  • Limited competition: Only a few GLP-1 drugs exist
  • Manufacturing costs: Biologics are complex to produce (though estimates suggest actual production cost is as low as $5/month)
